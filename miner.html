How to Play Arrow Keys / A,D to run  •  Up / W / Space to jump (only while grounded)
Desktop: Left-click a block to mine it, right-click to flag it
Touch: Left/Right + Jump buttons to move, tap a block to mine it (or flag it, with Flag Mode on)
15% of the ground hides bombs — mining one ends the game. Flagged blocks stay solid and safe.
