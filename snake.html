🐍 SNAKE GAME

by Ofir Yehonatan
How to Play
Use Arrow Keys or WASD to move the snake (or the on-screen D-pad on touch devices)
Space to pause/resume  •  Walls and grey rocks are now deadly — no more wraparound!
Gold food is a timed bonus, purple food is poison — eat fast for combo bonuses!
An AI rival shares the board racing for the same food — its trail is deadly, but crashing it earns you bonus points!
